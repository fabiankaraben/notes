
Con "he", "she" e "it" usamos "doesn't".

| Person           | Verbo         |
| ---------------- | ------------- |
| I, you, we, they | don't speak   |
| he, she, it      | doesn't speak |

### Formas afirmativa y negativa de "to speak" en presente.

| Persona          | Verbo (+) | Verbo (-)     |
| ---------------- | --------- | ------------- |
| I, you, we, they | speak     | don't speak   |
| he, she, it      | speaks    | doesn't speak |
